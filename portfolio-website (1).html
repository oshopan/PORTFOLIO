IOT Based Electric Vehicle Battery Management System

Team Members: Osho Pandey

Guided by: Prof. Sushant Kumar

Overview

This project tackles EV battery safety through real-time monitoring and multi-level cooling mechanisms to prevent overheating and potential hazards like battery explosions.

Features

  • Real-time monitoring via mobile app
  • Alarm and notification at 75°C
  • Air cooling at 80°C
  • Fluid cooling at 85°C
  • System shutdown at 90°C
  • Automated stepwise restoration

Tools & Components: ESP8266, DHT-11, Relay Units, Cooling Fan, Fluid Pump, MOSFETs, etc.
